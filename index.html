Three activities to do in Macau

Macau Central

This area is the busiest corner in Macau. It is full of hight clas shops selling famous branded items and nice restaurants offering lot of delicious food

Macau Beach

This is the largest beach in Macau. The sand there is dark and there is a famous Portuguese restaurant in the location. During weekend, you will see people flying beautiful kites over the sky.

Macau Cuisine

Popular dishes include galinha à Portuguesa, galinha à Africana (African chicken), bacalhau (traditional Portuguese salt cod), pato de cabidela, Macanese chili shrimps, minchi, stir-fried curry crab; pig's ear and papaya salad, and rabbit stewed in wine, cinnamon and star anise.
